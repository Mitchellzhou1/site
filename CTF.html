What are CTFs?

CTF competitions typically feature a range of cybersecurity challenges,
including cryptanalysis, binary exploitation, web exploitation, and
forensics. These challenges often involve solving puzzles, finding
vulnerabilities in software, analyzing network traffic, or decrypting
hidden messages. Participants must apply their technical skills and
problem-solving abilities to overcome these hurdles and capture
"flags" to earn points. Learn more

We are a part of the University's Center for Cyber Security. Our Capture The Flag team NYUSEC competes in cyber security competitions every weekend.
